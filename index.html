About Schmidt's Home Services

Schmidt's Home Services is a family-owned company with over 15 years of experience in the construction industry. The owners, Joshua, Penny, Melody, and Gibson, founded Schmidt's after moving to Florida from Michigan at the end of 2019. Joshua maintains current business operations while Penny has migrated toward the technical side of things, and the children handle project review at the end of each day. The whole family takes a role in every project. While the kiddos may just be telling Mommy and Daddy if they like the final product or not, they have their role.

Here at Schmidt's, we do the work so you don't have to. We have serviced a lot of folks around the Florida area and have received impressive reviews on our exceptional work. In these uncertain times, wouldn't it be nice to have just one company that can handle all of your household service needs? Stop rotating through company after company with what seems like never-ending streams of workers in and out of your home. Avoid the hassle and hire Schmidt's Home Services to get the job done quickly and accurately the first time!
